# piece-yen.github.io

## Website Summary

1. This is my research portfolio.
2. I am Piece Yen, a multidisciplinary neuroscientist with training in molecular biology, systems neuroscience, and auditory physiology. I use electrophysiology and computational analysis to study mammalian auditory systems, with a focus on age-related and noise-induced hearing loss.
3. My research investigates activity-dependent neuroplasticity in the ageing auditory system, aiming to identify early points of failure and therapeutic windows. Current work focuses on early cellular markers in ARHL, distinct NIHL/ARHL trajectories with shared targets, and methods/reproducibility in auditory science.
