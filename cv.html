Appointments

Research Associate, School of Biosciences, University of Sheffield (April 2023 – September 2025)
  • Supervisor: Dr. Stuart Johnson
  • Investigating the physiology of accelerated progression of age-related hearing loss after temporary noise-induced hearing loss in early-adult mice.
Research Associate, Institute of System Neuroscience, National Tsing Hua University (October 2013 – September 2015)
  • Supervisor: Dr. Chung-chuan Lo
  • Project 1: Multi-scale interactions between pre- and post-synapses shapes transcranial magnetic stimulation-induced long-term plasticity (grant-funded).
    • Developed a calcium-dependent plasticity (CaDP) model at single synapse and network scale.
    • Predicted the plasticity induced by novel TBS pattern.
  • Project 2: Short-term spatial memory in un-trained Drosophila melanogaster.
    • Designed and built a customized experimental apparatus.

Education

PhD, Biomedical Sciences, The University of Sheffield (2018 – 2022)
  • Thesis: The influence of non-sensory cells on the development of mammalian inner hair cells.
  • Provided evidence that ATP signaling, without potassium secretion, from non-sensory cells is sufficient to drive functional maturation in cochlear hair cells.
MSc, Systems Neuroscience, National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) (2010 – 2012)
  • GPA: 4.1/4.3
  • Thesis: A Spiking Neural Network Model of Motor Cortex for Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation-Induced Synaptic Plasticity.
  • Built a large-scale neural network model of primary motor cortex to simulate synaptic plasticity and studied candidate mechanisms of TMS-induced synaptic plasticity.
BSc, Life Science, National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) (2006 – 2010)
  • GPA: 76.89/100
